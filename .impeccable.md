## Design Context

### Users

The primary audience is pharmaceutical partners, investors, bankers, scientific reviewers, and other stakeholders conducting strategic diligence on EndoCyclic Therapeutics. They need to understand the platform differentiation, lead-program stage, pipeline breadth, external validation, and partnership path quickly and with confidence.

### Brand Personality

Precise, calm, and consequential. The experience should feel scientifically credible and institutionally mature while still making the platform's selective mechanism memorable. Confidence should come from clarity, restraint, and evidence rather than promotional intensity.

### Aesthetic Direction

Use the Calm Clinical foundation: soft off-white and cream grounds, muted plum ink, clinical teal, restrained gold, and Hanken Grotesk. The flagship creative direction is "The Selective Thread": one authored peptide-like line connects disease burden, mechanism, programs, and validation through a cinematic editorial composition. It may feel Awwwards-level and technically ambitious, but it must not resemble a generic neon biotech site, a dashboard, a card template, or decorative science fiction. No WebGL, scroll-jacking, oversized type walls, or unqualified scientific claims.

### Design Principles

1. Make the selective mechanism the visual protagonist; decoration must reinforce scientific meaning.
2. Present one clear diligence question per chapter and keep every claim traceable to `truth.md`.
3. Prefer a few authored, high-impact compositions over repeated cards, badges, and generic entrance effects.
4. Keep native scrolling, reduced-motion support, and a Lighthouse 90+ target; motion uses transform, opacity, and clip-path only.
5. Preserve decisive CTA hierarchy: partnership first, investor and pipeline paths secondary.
