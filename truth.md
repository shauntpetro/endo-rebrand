## Disease Burden (Endometriosis)
- 190 million+ women affected worldwide
- 10% of reproductive-age women globally
- $200 billion annual economic burden (US)
- Global market potential for endometriosis treatments: $180 billion to $250 billion (McKinsey estimate)
- 8-year average diagnostic delay
- Leading cause of infertility and chronic pelvic pain
- Chronic disease characterized by growth of endometrial-like tissue outside the uterus
- Associated comorbidities: cardiovascular disease, increased risk of certain cancers, other inflammatory conditions
- Current therapies are largely hormone-based, symptomatic, and do not eliminate lesions or modify disease biology
